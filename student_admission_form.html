Welcome to St Francis SSS Busunju below are our rules and regulations

CODE RULES AND REGULATIONS STIPULATED PUNISHMENT A GENERAL RULES.

 
1. Insolence of any nature to the school administration, members of teaching and non- teaching staff and students’ leaders is strictly forbidden Expulsion
2. Physical violence, teasing, bullying and unruly behavior are strictly prohibited Expulsion
3. Stealing, robbery and forgery or cheating of any kind is strongly prohibited. Expulsion
4. Rioting of any nature (attempting to incite a riot) is typical of an uncivilized community. Expulsion
5. Alcohol, drugs and smoking are strictly forbidden Expulsion
6. Promiscuous behaviors such as boy- girl/ boy- boy/ girl- girl relationships associated with immorality are strongly condemned. Expulsion
7. Escapism is a very grave offence Suspension of not more than one week and reporting back with a reel of chain- link- 12 or expulsion
8. Use of obscene and abusive language is strictly forbidden Punishable suspension for a month
9. Military uniform or equipment i.e. bullets, pistols, guns, Shoes, e.t.c must not be brought to school Expulsion
10. Staff- quarters are strictly out of bounds to learners except in case of emergencies Suspension for a month
11. Possession of electric gadgets such as coils, water- heaters, shavers or communication equipment like phones, radios, ipads, e.t.c or photographic equipment such as cameras is prohibited Confiscation and destruction of the equipment and suspension for a week.
12. Sharing of properties like beds, clothes, face- masks, shoes, sandals, meal- cards, fees clearance cards, e.t.c is not allowed Confiscation of the shared item and manual work of not more than one week to both the lender and the borrower.
13. Painting of finger and toe- nails and decolourization of one’s skin are not allowed Manual work of not more than a week after immediate removal of the paint from the nails.

14. Possession of pornographic materials i.e. Magazines, Newspapers, e.t.c is highly condemned. Expulsion
15. Throwing stones, sticks is highly punishable Can lead to expulsion
16. Fruits, flowers, fences and plants on the school compound are school property. No student should attempt to pick or break them. Can lead to expulsion
17. Negligence of personal property is highly punishable. Can lead to suspension Riding bicycles, motor cycles, cars, e.t.c on the school compound is highly condemned. Learners should alight from them and roll them gently to the parking yard and the gate. Confiscation of the misused locomotive for a month.B UNIFORM AND UNIFORMITY
18. Shortening, lengthening of the skirt or tampering with the school uniform in any way, and lowering of trousers below the waist level is forbidden. Confiscation of the modified uniform and buying a new one
19. Ear rings, necklaces, nail vanish, rings, lip stick, bangles, bracelets, capes, hats are not allowed Complete confiscation, Suspension for a month or expulsion
20. Students’ hair should kept at ¼ (a quarter an inch), French cut, back- bush and shaolin are forbidden Highly punishable by suspension for a month
21. Learners must not wear unauthorized jackets, cardigans, sweaters, head soak, coats, scuffs, casual- wears, uniforms for other schools, e.t.c Confiscation for good
22. Irregularity in school attendance, failure to produce classroom notes, assignments, holiday- work, e.t.c are prohibited. Reprimand, manual work of not more than one week or suspension.
23. The English language is the medium of communication. Vernacular speaking is indistinguishable from sectarianism in this multilingual society. Suspension for a month
24. All learners must purchase their requirements only from the school canteen during break time, lunch time and after classes. Manual work of not more than one week, reprimand or suspension.
25. All learners must be dressed in full school uniform, walk and talk in an organized and orderly manner at, to and fro school Manual work of not more than one week, reprimand or suspension.
26. All learners going out for games and sports, seminars, trips, tours, e.t.c must be in full school uniform and must carry their identity cards with them for security purposes. Shouting and nasty singing are not allowed in the school bus/ truck Expulsion
27. Vandalism and malicious damaging of school property i.e. writing on desks, walls of classrooms and toilets, breaking desks, sockets, waste- bins, window panels, fire extinguishers, e.t.c is highly forbidden. Suspension of not more than two weeks and reporting back to school after replacing six- fold.
28. Leaving the school campus without written permission is highly condemned. Self- dismissal from school. C EXAMINATION/ ACADEMICS
29. All learners must observe double margins and should use indented system of paragraphing. Reprimand and manual work for a week
30. Cheating of any nature in examination is not allowed Instant demotion irrespective of class or term
31. After an examination period, learners must continue with the normal school routine i.e. class- break- lunch- games and preps. Suspension for a month
32. Learners must observe sitting plan instructions and directions governing exams Suspension for a month
33. Learners with chronic illnesses must report to the deputy administration with medical documents from qualified medical personnel. Expulsion
34. Preps are mandatory to every boarding learner. They must have their preps in appropriate rooms allocated to them at the right time. Reprimand and manual work for a week
35. Learners must observe library and laboratory hours and rules. Learners must take proper care of all library and laboratory materials in their care. Materials lost or damaged must be replaced six- fold.
36. Missing class is punishable. In case of sickness, permission should be obtained from the administration Suspension or expulsion
37. Missing or missing exams is not allowed Self- dismissal
38. Shouting or any other form of noise which disrupts the freedom of others is highly prohibited Manual work of not more than one week, reprimand or suspension.
39. Unnecessary movement during class, Preps and other school activities is prohibited. Manual work of not more than one week, reprimand or suspension.
D DOMESTIC RULES 40. All learners must report to school on the first day of the term before 5:00 pm. Receiving those who have not complied on the weekend.
41. The staff- room is out of bounds to learners. Suspension for a month.
42. All societies/ Clubs shall have a patron who is a member of teaching staff.
43. Society leaders must not communicate with outside bodies/ personalities through their patrons/ club leaders. All correspondences from societies or persons must be addressed through the head teacher of the school to the head teacher of the specific school concerned and must be counter- signed by the patron of the society.
44. All societies/ clubs shall not mobilize money from club members without the consent of the patron.
45. No society shall make a trip without the patron. However, the patron may request (a) member(s) of the academic staff to accompany the club on his/ her behalf.
46. House- work and general cleaning are a must to all students without exception Manual work of not more than one week, reprimand or suspension.
47. Learners must leave the dormitory and the dormitory vicinity- compound, verandah by 7:00 am. Any learner found there beyond this time commits an offence. Manual work of not more than one week, reprimand or suspension.
48. Dormitories shall be out of bounds to learners during class and prep time Two weeks’ suspension
49. Attendance of mass, recollection and other spiritual animation activities is a must to all students. Suspension or manual work of not more than one week or reprimand.
50. Learners are not allowed in the kitchen before the time for serving meals. Learners should not serve themselves meals. Suspension for a month and coming back with a fifty kilogram sack of maize flour.
51. Mudslinging, rumor mongering and back biting are highly punishable Manual labor or suspension for a month.
52. All learners must ensure proper hygiene and care for the safety of all. Any learner who does anything that endangers the safety of self or others by being unhygienic commits an offence. Reprimand and manual labor for a week
53. Learners must have personal jerry cans and basins/ buckets. Reprimand
54. Learners must attend all religious ceremonies at the school regardless of religious differences i.e. Mass, Adoration, Evening Prayers, Recollection, Way of the Cross, e.t.c Suspension for a month or expulsion.
55. Shouting and any form of noise- making which may disturb the freedom of others is forbidden, more especially the eve of the closing day of the term “kirommo” Suspension for a month or expulsion.
56. All forms of private cooking are not allowed at school. Cooking facilities i.e. stoves, coils, saucepans, percolators. E,t,c are not allowed at school. Suspension for a month.
57. Classes, compound, toilets and dormitories are cleaned by learners. Any learner, who absconds from cleaning on the day he / she is programmed for it on the Rota, commits an offence. Manual labor for a month.
58. The girls’ dormitory is out of bounds to boys and vice- versa. Students found without the knowledge of the administration will be expelled.
59. Attendance of assemblies and class- meetings is a must to all students. Reprimand, manual work of not more than one week or suspension.
60. It is a criminal offence for a student to operate electricity. Suspension of not more than two weeks or expulsion.
61. Punctuality for all school functions is a must to all students. Reprimand or manual work of not more than one week.
62. On reporting for a new term, all students must register with the relevant authorities and come in school uniform. Reprimand, suspension of not more than one week or expulsion.
63. It is a must for all students to put on a proper school uniform all the time. Reprimand, suspension of not more than one week.
64. On the days when a dance is offered, all learners must dance in a formal way as expected of them as learners. Any form of immoral dancing such as those where learners of either opposite or same sex stick to each other that tarnish the name of the school is strongly condemned. Suspension for two weeks.
E PREFECTS’ LEADERSHIP CODES
64. It is highly condemned for a prefect to insult, embarrass or demoralize a fellow prefect Instant demotion and dismissal from school
65. Prefect leaders are at all times supposed to be exemplary to the rest of the members in everything. Reprimand.
66. All prefects must be loyal to the school philosophy, mission, and motto and academically sounding. Reprimand or demotion or both.
67. There should be no leakage of information from prefects’ meeting/ discussion to the students’ community. Instant demotion.
68. Teamwork is a must; remember there is no “I” but “WE” in TEAMWORK! and in a sinking- boat, there is no winner Reprimand
F VISITATION/ VISITATION DAYS
69. Parents are encouraged to visit their children on all Visitation Days and provide them with necessary basic requirements.
70. Cooked food is not allowed for the safety of our learners. Expulsion
71. Visiting hours are strictly between 9:00 am and 5:00 pm.
72. All parents must strictly observe office working hours and days i.e. 8:00 am- 5:00 pm from Monday to Friday.
73. Parents/ Guardians and relatives are not allowed to see learners during school time. Suspension of learner with the parent for a month.
74. Visitors must seek permission from school administrators before talking to any learner. Learner will be sent home with the visitors.
75. Visitors are not allowed in dormitories. Learner will be suspended with the visitor.
76. Parents should only come to school when they are decently dressed. Indecently dressed parents will not be allowed in school ( refer to the chart for acceptable dress- code).
77. Learners shall only use the parents’ or guardians’ mobile phones when they are near them. Parents will not allow learners to wander far away around school from them with their phones. Confiscation for good.
